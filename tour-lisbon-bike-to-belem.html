Lisbon Bike Tour to Belém

Guided small-group bike tour from the city centre to Belém. The route is mostly downhill and flat, following calmer streets and riverside paths with regular stops for views and stories.

Key facts

  • Duration: about 3.5 hours including stops
  • Group size: 4–12 guests
  • Languages: EN / NL (one language per group)
  • Route profile: 50/50 downhill and flat

Pricing

  • €39 per person (VAT excluded)
  • Minimum 4 paying guests to operate the tour
  • Maximum 12 guests per guide (second guide policy to be defined)

Highlights

  • City overview from the Parque Eduardo VII / Marquês de Pombal area.
  • Gentle descent along or near Avenida da Liberdade into downtown Lisbon.
  • Short stop in Baixa and Praça do Comércio by the river.
  • Flat riverside ride past Cais do Sodré and the Time Out Market area.
  • Views towards the 25 de Abril Bridge and Cristo Rei.
  • Belém waterfront with Monument to the Discoveries, Belém Tower surroundings and Jerónimos exterior.
  • Snack or coffee stop in Belém before the tour ends there.

Sample itinerary

Order and exact route may change after test rides and according to traffic, events and weather.

  • 00:00 – Meet & bike setup – Welcome, bike fitting, helmets and safety briefing.
  • 00:20 – City overview – Viewpoint stop near Parque Eduardo VII / Marquês de Pombal.
  • 00:40 – Descent into the centre – Controlled downhill ride with stops at safe points.
  • 01:10 – Baixa & Praça do Comércio – Short off-bike stop to introduce downtown Lisbon.
  • 01:30 – Riverside ride to Cais do Sodré – Easy flat cycling, focusing on group formation.
  • 02:00 – Riverside route to Belém – Longer flat section with 1–2 photo or drink stops.
  • 02:40 – Belém monuments area – Off-bike exploration around the main monuments.
  • 03:10 – Snack / coffee stop – Light food and drink in Belém (partner to be confirmed).
  • 03:30 – End of tour in Belém – Bikes returned, guidance for tram/train/taxi back.

Bikes & safety

  • Standard city bikes included; e-bikes may be added later as an upgrade.
  • Helmets provided and strongly recommended for all guests.
  • Route prioritises quieter streets and bike paths where possible.
  • Safety briefing covers braking, signals, formation and emergency stop.

What is included

  • Guided 3.5-hour bike tour with a trained guide.
  • Standard city bike and helmet for the duration of the tour.
  • Planned short snack or coffee stop in Belém area (details to follow).
  • Support for basic adjustments and minor issues during the ride.

What is not included

  • Hotel pickup and drop-off.
  • E-bike upgrade (if offered later).
  • Extra food and drinks beyond the agreed snack / coffee stop.
  • Entrance tickets to museums or monuments in Belém.
  • Transport back to the city centre after the tour.

Know before you go

  • Guests must already know how to ride a bike confidently.
  • Average fitness is enough; there are no long climbs in the standard route.
  • Route may be adjusted or cancelled in case of heavy rain or strong winds.
  • Minimum age and child bike/seat policy will be defined before launch.
  • Please wear closed shoes and comfortable, weather-appropriate clothing.
