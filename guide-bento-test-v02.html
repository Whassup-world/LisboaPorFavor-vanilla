Why I guide in Lisbon, what I love about the city, and what to expect from a tour with me.

Welcome to LXTourguide, your local expert in Lisbon. I'm George, your dedicated tour guide, partnering with LisboaPorFavor to offer personalized walking and biking tours that showcase the best of our beautiful city.

On a typical day together we might start in a historic neighbourhood, stop for a coffee or pastel de nata where locals actually go, and finish at a viewpoint or riverside spot that feels like "your" Lisbon.

Why choose LXTourguide?

  • Customized tours designed around what you care about most.
  • Local insights and stories you won't find in a standard guidebook.
  • Small groups and a relaxed pace, so you can really enjoy the city.
