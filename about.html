Our Story

Founded in 2024, LisboaPorFavor was born from a passion for sharing the authentic beauty of Lisbon. Our founders, longtime Lisbon residents, noticed that many visitors were missing out on the city's true character by following generic tourist paths.

Today, we work with a carefully selected network of local guides who share our commitment to providing meaningful, memorable experiences that go beyond the typical tourist attractions.
