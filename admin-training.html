Tour Training Overview

Quick overview for admins and staff of the current training levels and documents for the Lisbon - Alfama Vintage Market Walking Tour With Tastings and the Belém bike tour.

Open guide-facing training page Lisbon - Alfama Vintage Market Walking Tour With Tastings (MD) Belém bike tour spec (MD) Training levels outline (MD)

Tour

Lisbon - Alfama Vintage Market Walking Tour With Tastings

3 hours · 4–8 guests · EN / NL · Runs on Tuesdays and Saturdays only. Food, viewpoints and vintage market walk through Graça & Alfama with 3–4 tastings.

  • Beginner: can assist on route and explain basic food & history.
  • Advanced: can lead full tour unsupervised.
  • Expert: can train others and design variations.

Tour

Lisbon Bike Tour to Belém

3.5 hours · 4–12 guests · EN / NL · Mostly downhill and flat, with a focus on safe group riding.

  • Beginner: understands route and basic bike safety.
  • Advanced: can lead groups and manage mixed abilities.
  • Expert: can mentor juniors and handle special/large groups.
