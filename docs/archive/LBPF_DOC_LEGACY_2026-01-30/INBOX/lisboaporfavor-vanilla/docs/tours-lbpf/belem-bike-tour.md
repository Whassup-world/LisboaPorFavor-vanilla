# Lisbon Bike Tour to Belém

## 1. Summary

Guided small-group bike tour from Lisbon city centre to Belém. The route is designed to be mostly downhill and flat, so average biking skills and condition are enough. You follow safe streets and riverside paths, with regular stops for views, stories and photos.

### 1.1 Key facts

- Duration: about 3.5 hours including stops
- Group size: minimum 4, maximum 12 guests
- Language: EN / NL  (one language per group)
- Terrain: mostly downhill from Parque Eduardo VII / Marquês de Pombal area to the river, then flat along the Tagus
- Bikes: comfortable city bikes as standard; e-bike upgrades can be offered later if needed

## 2. Why book this tour

- See much more of Lisbon in half a day than you can on foot.
- Combine city views, riverside cycling and the monuments of Belém.
- Route chosen to avoid heavy traffic where possible and favour bike lanes / quieter streets.
- Regular stops so the tour stays accessible for average cyclists.
- Small groups so the guide can look after everyone’s safety.

## 3. Highlights (for cards and SEO)

- Start near Parque Eduardo VII / Marquês de Pombal with a first view over the city.
- Glide down Avenida da Liberdade and into downtown Lisbon.
- Cross through Baixa towards Praça do Comércio and the riverfront.
- Follow the riverside past Cais do Sodré and the Time Out Market area.
- Views towards the 25 de Abril Bridge and Cristo Rei statue.
- Belém waterfront and monuments area (Monument to the Discoveries, Belém Tower surroundings, Jerónimos exterior area).
- Snack or coffee stop in Belém area (exact partner venue to be confirmed).

## 5. Bikes, safety and fitness

- **Bikes:** standard city bikes suitable for urban riding; e-bikes can be offered later as an upgrade if demand exists.
- **Route profile:** roughly 50/50 downhill and flat. No long climbs built into the standard route.
- **Required skill level:** comfortable starting, stopping, turning and riding in a straight line on a bike; no technical mountain biking.
- **Required fitness:** average condition is enough for 3.5 hours with multiple stops.
- **Equipment:** helmets provided and strongly recommended; guests advised to bring water, sun protection and weather-appropriate clothing.
