## 1.0.0 MARKETING STRATEGY

### **Month 1-3: Build Foundation**

**SEO (Primary Channel)**
- Publish 15-20 posts/month
- Target long-tail keywords
- Build internal linking
- Submit to Google Search Console

**Social Media (Instagram Focus)**
- Daily stories (behind the scenes)
- 4-5 feed posts per week
- Reels showcasing tours, food, art
- Use hashtags: #LisboaPortugal #LisbonTourGuide #VisitLisbon

**Google My Business**
- Claim "LisboaPorFavor" listing
- Collect reviews from tour customers
- Post weekly updates
- Add all tours as services

**Content Expansion**
- Increase to 25-30 posts/month
- Add video content (YouTube)
- Create downloadable guides (lead magnets)
- Guest posts on travel blogs

**Partnerships**

## 1.2.0 CONTENT SYSTEM

### **Blog Post Templates**

#### **Template 1: Food Guide Post**

**AI Prompt:**
```
Write a blog post about [DISH NAME] in Lisbon.

Structure:
1. Captivating intro (why this dish is special)
2. History and cultural significance
3. What makes a great [DISH]
4. Where to find the best ones in Lisbon (3-5 spots)
5. Insider tips from a local
6. How to order like a local
7. Call to action (book food tour)

Tone: Friendly local guide, conversational, authentic
Length: 800-1000 words
Include: Personal anecdotes, hidden gems, practical tips
SEO: Natural keywords for "Lisbon [dish]" "best [dish] Lisbon"

Title: "The Ultimate Guide to [DISH] in Lisbon: Local's Secrets"
```

#### **Template 2: Historical Content**

**AI Prompt:**
```
Write comprehensive guide about [HISTORICAL TOPIC] in Lisbon.

Structure:
1. Hook (fascinating fact or question)
2. Historical background
3. What happened and why it matters
4. How it shaped modern Lisbon
5. What you can see/visit today
6. Insider perspective from tour guide
7. How to experience it on our tours

Tone: Educational but engaging, storytelling approach
Length: 1000-1200 words
Include: Timeline, interesting details tourists don't know
SEO: "[Topic] Lisbon" "History of [topic]"

Title: "[TOPIC]: The Story That Changed Lisbon Forever"
```

#### **Template 3: Street Art Guide**

**AI Prompt:**
```
Write about [STREET ARTIST] and their work in Lisbon.

Structure:
1. Who is [Artist]? (background)
2. Their signature style
3. Famous works in Lisbon (locations)
4. The story behind their art
5. Where to find their pieces
6. Best street art tours to see them
7. Photography tips

Tone: Hip, artistic, local insider knowledge
Length: 700-900 words
Include: Exact locations, best times to visit, photo spots
SEO: "[Artist] Lisbon" "Lisbon street art"

Title: "[ARTIST]: Lisbon's [Description] - Complete Guide"
```
